## Executive Summary

PropMaster is positioned as a **feature-complete, enterprise-grade** property management platform that matches or exceeds the top 5 industry leaders (AppFolio, Buildium, DoorLoop, Rentvine, LOFT/RealPage) in most categories. With 21+ major features, an advanced accounting module (Titanium v7.2), and four complete portals, PropMaster is ready for aggressive market entry starting January 2026.

### Quick Stats for Pitches:
- **Market Size (2025):** $26.55 billion globally, $10.22B North America
- **Market Growth:** 10.1% CAGR through 2032 (~$52B)
- **Target ARR Year 1:** $2.1 million
- **Target Customers Year 1:** 800 PMCs managing 60,000 units
- **Unique Advantage:** Only PM software with event-sourced accounting + predictive maintenance ML

## 5. Pricing Strategy

### Recommended Model: Aggressive Flat-Rate + Per-Unit Hybrid

**Strategic Rationale:**
1. **Undercut AppFolio's 50-unit minimum** to capture SME market
2. **Match Rentvine's all-inclusive philosophy** (no feature paywalls)
3. **Be more predictable than Buildium** (no surprise add-ons)
4. **Scale better than DoorLoop** at high unit counts
