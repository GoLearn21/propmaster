## Investor FAQ

**Q: How are you different from AppFolio?**
A: Three key differences: (1) No 50-unit minimum - we serve all PMCs, (2) Event-sourced accounting architecture for better auditing, (3) 30-50% lower pricing with no feature paywalls.

**Q: Why will customers switch?**
A: Price fatigue (annual increases), feature gaps, and our free migration program with 90-day parallel runs.

**Q: What's your CAC strategy?**
A: Product-led growth with freemium (5 units free), combined with content marketing targeting "AppFolio alternative" searches.

**Q: How defensible is your technology?**
A: Our Titanium Accounting engine is event-sourced with saga-based workflows - fundamentally different architecture that would take competitors 2-3 years to replicate.

**Q: What about AppFolio's AI (Realm-X)?**
A: They're focused on leasing automation. We're focused on predictive maintenance and accounting intelligence - different use cases, both valuable.

**Q: What's your churn expectation?**
A: Industry average is 5% monthly. We're targeting <3% through superior onboarding and customer success.
